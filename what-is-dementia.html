What is Dementia

Dementia is a syndrome associated with an ongoing decline of cognitive function. Learn more about its causes, symptoms, and support.

Definition

Dementia is not a specific disease but a group of symptoms affecting memory, thinking, and social abilities severely enough to interfere with daily life.

Causes

Common causes include Alzheimer's disease, vascular dementia, and Lewy body dementia.

Symptoms

  • Memory loss
  • Difficulty communicating
  • Confusion
  • Mood changes

Support

Early diagnosis and support can improve quality of life. Seek medical advice and join support communities.
