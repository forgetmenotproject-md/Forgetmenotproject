Care for Dementia

Resources and tips for caregivers supporting loved ones with dementia.

View our full Communication Tips Playlist

Communication

Effective communication is essential when caring for someone with dementia. Our video series provides practical strategies for connecting with and supporting your loved one.

Caregiving Strategies

Effective ways to provide care, including communication tips and daily routines.

  • Use simple language and maintain eye contact.
  • Create a safe environment by removing hazards.
  • Establish routines to reduce confusion.

Emotional Support

Taking care of your own mental health while caregiving.

Join support groups and seek professional help when needed.

Daily Care Tips

Practical advice for bathing, feeding, and medication management: use a shower stool for safety and for greater independence, offer smaller meals more frequently, and use a pill organizer.
